Andhra University is a public university located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. It was established in 1926 and is one of the oldest universities in the country. The university is spread over a large campus of over 350 acres and includes several faculties, departments, and research centers.

The university has several modern and well-equipped infrastructure facilities, including classrooms, laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, hostels, and canteens. There are also several auditoriums and conference halls on campus for hosting events and seminars.

The library at Andhra University is one of the largest in the state and houses a large collection of books, journals, and other research materials. The university also has several computer centers and internet facilities, making it easy for students to access information and resources.

In addition to its academic facilities, the university also has a hospital and a dispensary on campus to provide healthcare services to students and faculty.

Overall, Andhra University has a strong infrastructure that supports the teaching, learning, and research activities of the university.
